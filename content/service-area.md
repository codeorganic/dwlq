I'm based in **Des Moines, Washington**, just south of Seattle.

## How to get your quilt to me

- **Drop off in Des Moines** by appointment. Reach out through the contact form and we'll find a time that works.
- **Pick up at a Western Washington quilt show, guild meeting, or shop event** — I'm at a number of these each year. Mention it on the contact form if there's one you'll attend, and I can let you know if I'll be there.
- **Ship your quilt to me.** Most quilters use USPS Priority with insurance. I'll send the finished quilt back the same way (the return shipping cost is added to your invoice at actual price).
